Build modern, responsive websites with no hassle

A cool and stunning CSS remix of Replit.com, with similar color palettes and components to fit any website and design.

Easily customize your components

With our built-in CSS variables, you can simply change anything, from background colors to height values.

:root {
  --background-root: #2B3245;
  --alert-width: 35px;
  --accent-primary-dimmer: purple;
}

It's fully responsive

Using replit.css, your websites will all be responsive and are ensured to look good on any device. On mobile, tablet and laptop.
