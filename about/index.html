About

replit.css is a Replit-themed CSS library that follows similar components and color palettes to make your websites seem attached to Replit. I wanted to make this because I partially thought that Replit's CSS looked beautiful and that re-making such a thing for my personal websites and others would be a ripoff and awesome!

I definitely wanted to give users a taste of Replit when they use this library, which is why this follows similar color palettes just for the feel. The icon, text glow, navbar, buttons, inputs, etc. most of the components are made to look specifically like Replit in a way.

This project was definitely not possible without all those people who gave a helpful testimonial! Also, special shout-out to @sojs for creating a cool demo page. All of this is appreciated!
